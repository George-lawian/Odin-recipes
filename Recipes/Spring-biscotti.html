Recipe for Spring Biscotti

Fresh tasting biscotti with orange zest, dried cranberries, white chocolate, and pistachios. Very light and tasty.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon orange zest
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 egg white
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 ounces whitechocolate,chopped
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries
  • 1 1/4 cups pistachio nuts

steps

  1. step 1

    2. In a large bowl, cream together butter,sugar, orange zest, and vanilla with an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Mix in eggs and egg white one at a time, beating well aftereach addition. Sift together the flour, baking powder, and salt;gradually blend into the creamed mixure using a wooden spoon. Stir in the white chocolate, dried cranberries, and pistachios. Cover, and chill for 30 minuites, or until dough is no longer sticky.

  2. step 2

    3. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F(165 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  3. step 3

    4. Turn dough out on a lightly floured surface, and divide into halves. Form each half into a flattish log about 12 inches long by 3 inches wide. Arrange logs at least 3 inches apart on baking sheet.

  4. step 4

    5. Bake for 30 minuites in preheated oven, or until pale gold. Allow logs to cool on the baking sheet until cool enough to handle. On a cutting board, cut logs crosswise on a diagonal into 1 inch thich slices. Arrange on baking sheet.

  5. step 5

    6. Bake for am additional 15 minuites, until golden. Transfer biscotti to wire racks, and cool completely. Store in an air tight container at room temperature.
