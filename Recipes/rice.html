How to cook rice for 2 people

rice is an Asian dish that is rich in caborhydrates. Its well known to grow in swampy areas and the seeds only are consumed as food

Ingredients

  • rice half a kilogram
  • cooking oil
  • an onion
  • a tomato
  • blueband
  • salt half a tea spoon

steps

  1. Add cooking oil to a cooking pot under medium heat and let it cook for around 2-3 minuites
  2. slice the onions and add them to the oil and let them cook until they are golden brown.
  3. Chop the tomato into cubes and add to the onionns. Let them cook for 5 minuites until they form a paste
  4. Add salt to the paste
  5. add water and stir gently. cover the cooking pot with a lid until the water boils
  6. add the rice and let it cook until it is dry.
  7. add one table spoon of blueband and stir

Serve while hot.
